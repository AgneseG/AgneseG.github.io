Novel outcome measures in NPA

Developing new outcome measures based on 17 NPA (Neuropsychological Assessment) tests.
Analyses are based, for example, on the time between correct responses rather than
on the number of correct answers only. The focus is on the performance fluctuation over time,
with the goal to define the kind and gravity of each patient's impairment.

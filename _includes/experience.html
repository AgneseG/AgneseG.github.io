Next Empire (http://nextempire.net) is a digital product studio with many clients in the music business. For my audio segmentation project I’ve built from scratch an audio files classification neural networks model which segmented radiostreams into the individual songs and the speech or commercials in between. I used:
  • the HTML parser BeautifulSoup to retrieve data from remote websites;
  • librosa to decompose .mp3 files in spectrograms;
  • Python Keras to build the neural network classification model;
  • Changepoint Analysis to automate the classification process of the three categories.

Feb 2019 – Ongoing                                                                                                  Utrecht, NL
Cognitive impairments are currently assessed with a paper-and-pencil NPA (neuropsychological assessment). In collaboration with Philips Research a digital NPA platform has been developed, which allows for a more standardized administration, an automatized scoring and new outcome measures (e.g., strategy, fluctuations in performance). The focus of my project is to develop novel outcome measures to gain insight in participants’ working strategy and cognitive stability.
